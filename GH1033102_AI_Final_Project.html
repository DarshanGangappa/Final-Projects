Title: "Data Cleaning and Analysis of US airline flight routes and fares from the year 1993 to 2024 "

Introduction

The datasets which you have provided contain a structural data of US airline flight routes and fares from the year 1993 to 2024. It is made up of several indexes relating to flight connections, fares, airlines, as well as passengers traffic between different cities. Here we provide an introduction and the explanation of major fields in the dataset.

This dataset is about U. S. airline industry and can help to provide information on aspects as flight routes, fares and passengers for several decades. It is useful for evaluating the shift in the approach to ticket pricing, passengers behavior and the wars between full-service and LCC competitors. It has a wide temporal coverage and ranges from 1993 to 2024 which is useful in studying the technical changes that has occurred within the airline industry.

url for the data set: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhavikjikadara/us-airline-flight-routes-and-fares-1993-2024

# Identify numerical and categorical columns separately
numerical_cols = df.select_dtypes(include=['float64', 'int64']).columns
categorical_cols = df.select_dtypes(include=['object']).columns

# Impute missing values for numerical columns with median
for col in numerical_cols:
    if df[col].isnull().sum() > 0:
        df[col].fillna(df[col].median(), inplace=True)

# Impute missing values for categorical columns with mode
for col in categorical_cols:
    if df[col].isnull().sum() > 0:
        df[col].fillna(df[col].mode()[0], inplace=True)
